Corporate Profile

Who We Are

Savannah Electricity Distribution Company (Savannah EDC) is the specialized, modern utility framework established to handle retail energy distribution within the Gombe franchise perimeter. As a strategic subsidiary of Jos Electricity Distribution Company (JEDC), we combine regional legacy footprint stability with highly responsive, localized automation. Our mandate centers on grid clarity, aggressive modernization, and absolute pricing transparency.
