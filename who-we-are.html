Who We Are

Engineering the primary distribution systems across Gombe State.

Savannah Electricity Distribution Company (Savannah EDC) operates at the vital core of north-eastern Nigeria's energy network system. Established to deliver transparent, smart, and safe distribution metrics, we manage high-capacity infrastructure directly servicing industrial, commercial, and residential operational nodes.

Through strict adherence to NERC regulatory mandates, integration of structural data collection tools like our central Optima+ platforms, and alignment with national NEMSA standards, we remain dedicated to transforming volatile grid vectors into safe, dependable electrical assets.
